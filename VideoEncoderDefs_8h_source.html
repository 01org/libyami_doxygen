158 #ifdef __BUILD_GET_MV__
159  /*
160  * VideoEncMVBuffer is defined to store Motion vector.
161  * Memory size allocated to data pointer can be got by getMVBufferSize API.
162  * Picture is partitioned to 16*16 block, within the 16x16 block, each 4x4 subblock has the same MV.
163  * Access the first 4*4 subblock to get the MV of each 16*16 block, and skip the 16*16 block to
164  * get MV of next 16x16 Block.
165  * Refer to structure VAMotionVectorIntel about detailed layout for MV of 4*4 subblock.
166  * 16x16 block is in raster scan order, within the 16x16 block, each 4x4 block MV is ordered as below in memory.
167  * 16x16 Block
168  * -----------------------------------------
169  * | 1 | 2 | 5 | 6 |
170  * -----------------------------------------
171  * | 3 | 4 | 7 | 8 |
172  * -----------------------------------------
173  * | 9 | 10 | 13 | 14 |
174  * -----------------------------------------
175  * | 11 | 12 | 15 | 16 |
176  * -----------------------------------------
177  */
